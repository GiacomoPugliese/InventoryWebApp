Chemical Information Sheet

Get accurate nomenclature, essential facts, and comprehensive information for your reference needs.

Hydrochloric Acid (HCl)

Safety Measures

Wear appropriate protective eyeglasses or chemical safety goggles. Wear appropriate protective clothing to prevent skin exposure. Use a corrosion-resistant ventilation system.

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, dry, well-ventilated area away from incompatible substances. Keep containers tightly closed. Store protected from moisture. Corrosives area.

Sodium Hydroxide (NaOH)

Safety Measures

Wear protective gloves, clothing, and eye protection. Use only outdoors or in a well-ventilated area.

Storage Instructions

Store locked up in a dry, cool, and well-ventilated area. Keep container tightly closed when not in use.

Sulfuric Acid (H2SO4)

Safety Measures

Use personal protective equipment as required. Ensure adequate ventilation. Do not breathe dust/fume/gas/mist/vapours/spray.

Storage Instructions

Store in a corrosive resistant container with a resistant inner liner. Store locked up.

Nitric Acid (HNO3)

Safety Measures

Use personal protective equipment. Avoid breathing vapors, mist, or gas. Ensure adequate ventilation.

Storage Instructions

Keep in a tightly closed container, stored in a cool, dry, ventilated area. Protect against physical damage. Isolate from incompatible substances.

Ammonia (NH3)

Safety Measures

Wear protective gloves/protective clothing/eye protection/face protection. Use only outdoors or in a well-ventilated area.

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, well-ventilated place. Keep container tightly closed. Store locked up.

Acetone (C3H6O)

Safety Measures

Use personal protective equipment. Avoid breathing vapors, mist, or gas. Ensure adequate ventilation.

Storage Instructions

Keep containers tightly closed in a dry, cool, and well-ventilated place. Containers which are opened must be carefully resealed and kept upright to prevent leakage.

Ethanol (C2H5OH)

Safety Measures

Wear protective gloves/protective clothing/eye protection/face protection. Use only outdoors or in a well-ventilated area.

Storage Instructions

Keep away from heat/sparks/open flames/hot surfaces. Store in a well-ventilated place. Keep container tightly closed.

Methanol (CH3OH)

Safety Measures

Wear protective gloves/protective clothing/eye protection/face protection. Use only outdoors or in a well-ventilated area.

Storage Instructions

Keep containers tightly closed in a dry, cool and well-ventilated place. Keep away from heat, sparks, and open flames.
